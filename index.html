About Me

Hi there, I'm Mandy Zhou.

I'm a Computer Science student and researcher with a keen interest in data and computer engineering, as well as earth and space sciences.
While I occasionally dabble in art and design, my passion lies in using computer science to explore my creativity.

As a first-generation and woman in STEM, I am committed to making a difference in the field of engineering.
In working with semiconductors at Microchip Technology and research deep learning and VQA (Visual Question Answering) at Arizona State University, I hope to not only understand how to create and learn new technology, but how as an engineer, I can intertwine my creativity and knowledge to create inclusive technology and where I continue my research in machine learning, quantum computing, and remote sensing.
Although my career has just begun, I am excited about the possibilities and potential of my future and hope to continuously learn and make a difference within the community through my work.
