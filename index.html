About Me

Working behind the counter at my family's Chinese restaurant, a hub of customers coming in and out to enjoy a hot meal...

I had met a customer who talked about his incredible passion for the mathematical universe and his job "writing code", while enjoying a Shrimp Egg Foo Young.

I had asked, "What is 'writing code'?"

Unbeknownst to us and five years later,
I'm pursuing my B.S. in Computer Science from Arizona State University graduating in May 2024.

I currently intern at Microchip Technology as an Engineer Intern, learning about the semiconductor industry.
During the schoolyear, I am a Treasurer for Women in Science and Engineering at ASU.

As a first-generation college student, I'm passionate about sharing my goals and experiences
to encourage other women and underrepresented groups by shining a light on STEM, to dream big, and to be present.

Now, I'm taking my journey across the world - attending conferences such as TechUp For Women NYC and Harvard WECode, with a goal to make my way onto the speaker podium.
